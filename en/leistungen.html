Service 1

Niwaki (garden bonsai)

Care and shaping of garden trees, not pot bonsai. Common species include pine (Pinus), yew (Taxus baccata) and juniper (Juniperus). The goal is not a green wall, but a crown with space for light, air and long-term structure.

Send a Niwaki photo
Service 2

Japanese maples - Acer palmatum

Japanese maple and dissected maple forms need seasonal shaping, deadwood removal inside the crown, careful hand work against moss and fungal pressure, and architectural crown care before density becomes a visible problem.

Send a maple photo
Service 3

Conifers - especially pine (Pinus)

Scots pine (Pinus sylvestris), mountain pine (Pinus mugo), Japanese black pine (Pinus thunbergii), Japanese white pine (Pinus parviflora), Japanese red pine (Pinus densiflora), eastern white pine (Pinus strobus), yew (Taxus baccata), juniper (Juniperus), fir (Abies) and spruce (Picea). I cut selectively by hand, preserving future buds, light and air.
