Pinus parviflora: from dense mass to readable form

A Pinus parviflora work series: not simply cut smaller, but brought back into calm readable layers.

What was done
Dense areas were opened, distracting shoots were removed selectively and the cloud structure was restored.
Why
Light and air can move through the crown again, so the result looks cared for, not roughly clipped.
My value
My value is in reading the structure and keeping what the tree needs to grow well over the next years.
