What was done

Pinus parviflora: from delivery state to terrace niwaki

This series shows the same low Japanese white pine before placement and after it was set into the terrace garden.

What was done
The tree was set as a specimen in the prepared terrace planting; the result comes from position, proportion and calm integration with gravel, grasses and the house line.
Why
For a large garden bonsai, placement decides whether the low crown has enough room and whether the terrace reads as calm garden architecture.
My value
My value here is selection, proportion and placement: the tree needs to work in its setting, not only look good as a separate object.

Evergreen hedge: from dense block to controlled line

This series shows a real shaped hedge: dense mass, internal diagnosis, ladder work and a calmer line afterwards.

What was done
The overfull outer mass was corrected, the line was gathered and the dry inner area was documented as an important care signal.
Why
With dense conifers, cutting affects light, air and future recovery; a fast surface trim is not enough.
My value
My value is diagnosis before cutting: I look beyond the outside contour and read what is happening inside the plant.
